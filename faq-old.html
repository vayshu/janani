Frequently Asked Questions

Whistle bring people and their needs closer and faster.

Recently Asked Questions

Do I need to register for whistle app?

No. If you are an anonymous user, you don't need to register. Incase you want to search the whistles around you, then you can open the app, select the category, specify your needs and connect.

When you need to register?

You need to register only when you want to create whistle specifying your needs to your co-whistlers.

Can I increase my whistle search radius?

Yes. Your default radius is set to 10 kilo meters. You can set the radius anywhere from 1 km to 100 km. You can do this by going to menu, select settings and scroll radius in km to set your desired radius.

Can I restrict number of whistlers to appear in my search?

Yes. The default number of whistlers to appear in each search is set is 10. You can set the number of whistlers anywhere from 1 to 100. You can do this by going to menu, select settings and scroll limit to set your desired limit.

What is refresh interval?

The refresh interval is nothing but the system refreshes to get the latest whistlers around you based on the search condition. The default refresh interval is set to 30 seconds. You can set the refresh interval anywhere from 1 sec to 100 secs. You can do this by going to menu, select settings and scroll refresh interval in secs to set your desired limit.

Can user save preferences?

Yes. An user can save preferences by selecting any category and user type. For example, an user selects taxi as the category, "Looking for passenger?" as the user type and can save this as his/her preference. Next time when you open the app, you will be directly shown whistles of your preference saved.

Can I reset my preference?

Yes. You can do this by going to menu, select settings and uncheck preference set (ON/OFF) button.

Can I turn off whistler alert sound?

Yes. You can do this by going to menu, select settings and uncheck whistler alert (ON/OFF) button.

Can Whistler go invisible?

Yes. You can do this by going to menu, select settings and uncheck whistler visible (ON/OFF) button.

Does whistle app have any payment processing method?

No. It will be on the users’ own risk to pay for the service and get payment for the service. The Whistle does not collect money and it is not providing a guarantee for the money transaction of the service. It only provides tools to communicate with the payment system.

What are my choices to communicate with the whistler?

You can contact the whistler through sending SMS and/or calling the whistler phone number.

As a whistler, I don't want to receive calls or SMS. Does whistle support this feature?

Yes, Whistle supports this feature. There are two ways to do this.

  • While creating your profile itself, you can select either to show phone number or SMS option to others for communicating. Remember, at least one of the option must be selected.
  • You can also change this selection after creating your profile. Go to menu- settings - profile- change your option and click update profile button.
How to edit my profile information?

Go to menu- settings - profile- edit your information like name, photo and click update profile button.

Can I edit my phone number?

No you can't edit your phone number after registering. In case if you change your phone number, you the uninstall the app, reinstall it again and then register it with your new phone number.

Can I have images added while creating a whistle?

Yes, you can upload a maximum of 3 images per whistle.

Why I am not receiving the verification code through SMS?

Make sure your phone number follows the following format:

A" + " Symbol followed by country code followed by phone number

Eg., Valid USA phone number is +14083456789

Valid India phone number is +918123456789

Is it possible to get the verification code again?

Yes, you can resend the code again by click the resend the code button while registering with the app.

Can I give a thumbs up or thumbs down to a whistler more than once?

No, you can give a like only once to a whistler by clicking the thumbs up icon in the user profile. You can also unlike a liked whistler by clicking the thumbs down icon in the whistler profile. After an unlike of a whistler, you are allowed to like that whistler again. Therefore, at a given time you can't like a whistler more than once.

Can I rate the whistles created?

No, you can't rate whistles. You can only rate whistlers.

Can I do a voice search?

Yes, you can do a voice search by clicking the mike icon in the map page and speak your needs and the results will be displayed. You can also edit the text of what you have spoken which is displayed in the textbox near to the mike icon.

Can I revert the search selection?

Yes, you can revert the search by clicking the revert button in the map page. The search criteria will then be cleared.

Can I refresh my map without waiting for auto refresh?

Yes, you can refresh the map manually whenever you needed by clicking the refresh icon which is located on the top left corner of the map.

Can I search for whistles in a different location?

Yes, you can manually move the map to your desired location or by clicking zoom in/ zoom out button. Wait for the refresh to happen. You will see the matches if found.

What is the difference between anonymous user and registered user?

Anonymous user can only do a search Whereas registered user can create a whistle also.

I tried to create profile for whistle, but it didn’t work?

It’s possible that you created an profile in the past that’s still associated with your current phone number. If you don’t believe that you’ve created an account previously, please contact us and someone will help you out.

Why won’t the app find my location?

Your phone's GPS may not always be able to pinpoint your exact location. If you're indoors, your phone may have trouble establishing a good connection to the satellite(s) responsible for letting it find your location. The pin displayed in your app tells your location.

What are whistle hours of operation?

You can use whistle 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. Just open the app when you’re ready to create a whistle and we’ll connect you to the matching whistler nearby.

How does whistle monitor the quality of whistler?

Whistle regularly reviews all user ratings and feedback to ensure that we allow high quality, highly rated providers and subscribers to use our app. We actively seek feedback on every whistle, and take this feedback very seriously when evaluating our whistler.

Does whistle take me to the airport?

Absolutely! To make sure you will get to the airport in time, check the App ahead in time, so that you can reach on time.

Do I have to tip my service provider?

Being Whistler means there is no need to tip providers with any of our services.

How to add an image to my profile?

When creating a whistle, user can add an image to their profile by clicking the add image icon, select photo from your phone library or take a new picture using your phone camera, select the desired image and your image is added to the profile.

How do I get the app?

Download the Whistle app from the Google Play Store for android and iTunes for ios.

Can I use whistle without a smartphone?

Right now, whistle is only accessible through our Android app. We are always working on new ways to experience whistle.

How old do I have to be to have an whistle account?

You have to be 18 or older to have an whistle account.

Who are whistlers?

Whistlers are people in your community. We’ve whistlers from all walks of life like retirees, stay-at-home moms, professional drivers, college students etc all with great customer service skills and/or a good working vehicle.

Why are whistler ratings important?

Those with the best ratings are given preference in future. So remember to rate your whistler on each whistle. Whistle only maintains partnerships with those who consistently receive good ratings from users. That is how we reward great whistlers as well as ensure a positive user experience with each whistle.
