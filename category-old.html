Blood Donation

A blood donation occurs when a person voluntarily has blood drawn and used for transfusions. Blood banks often participate in the collection process as well as the procedures that follow it. The whistle app helps the blood banks to locate the blood donor who are nearest to the blood bank. Using the whistle app, the blood banks can whistle when they are in need of blood. They can also specify which blood group they are in need of while whistling itself. The members of whistle app who are logged into the app can see the whistle made by the blood banks. If any of the whistle member are interested in donating blood, then the blood bank and that particular member can make further conversation. Since the app helps to find the closest donor, this helps to save lives on time which is a very crucial factor in this category.
